Process Simulation Engineer

Inefficiencies cost time, money, and potential. Without simulation, businesses rely on trial and error, often making costly mistakes. Imagine designing an entire system, only to realize too late that a small oversight causes massive delays. Or worse—what if a process failure was preventable but wasn’t caught in time?

That’s where I come in. I use simulation to predict, optimize, and refine processes before they hit the real world. With the right models, we don’t just solve problems—we prevent them.
